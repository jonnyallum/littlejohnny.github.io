Meet Jonny

British festival energy with premium plates

I started catering in 2015 with Little Jonny's burger van making homemade burgers, then moved into pizzas and joint ventures with Noshbox founder John Brooks under Brothers Kitchen.

I ran the Fishbourne Centre, brought catering in-house, and launched weddings. Now I split my time between a care home role and events plus personal chef services—still chasing that fresh-off-the-grill buzz.

What drives us

  • Cooking live on-site so guests can smell and see the food
  • Homemade sauces, salads, and desserts baked fresh
  • Menus flex for dietary needs and budgets

Where we cover

Based in Hampshire—happy to travel for weddings, festivals, corporate launches, and garden parties.
