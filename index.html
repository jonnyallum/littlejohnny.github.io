Meet Jonny

A flavour-first journey that started in 2015

I launched Little Jonny's burger van back in 2015, crafting homemade burgers that quickly grew a loyal following. That passion evolved into pizzas and joint ventures with Noshbox founder John Brooks under Brothers Kitchen.

From there, I ran the Fishbourne Centre and brought catering in-house, adding weddings and large events to my repertoire. Today I split my time between caring work and delivering events, hog roasts, mobile bar service, and personal chef experiences across the region.

What drives us

  • Cooking live on-site so guests can smell and see the food
  • Homemade sauces, salads, and desserts baked fresh
  • Flexible menus that meet dietary needs and budgets

FAQ

How payments and bookings work

Do you take card payments?

Yes. We can provide a payment link for deposits or full package purchases, and our mobile bar is contactless-ready for guests.

Can I customise the menu?

Absolutely. Swap salads, add desserts, make it veggie or vegan friendly, or combine the hog roast with pizzas and the bar.

Do you travel outside Hampshire?

Yes—just share the venue postcode in the booking form and we'll include travel in the quote.

How soon should I book?

Weddings and summer weekends fill quickly. Submit the form or call, and we'll hold your date while we finalise the details.
